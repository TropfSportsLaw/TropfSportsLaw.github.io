About Us!

At Mountain Bean Cafe, we're more than just coffee-we're a local gathering place built on community, quality, and comfort. Since 2018, our mission has been to serve ethically sourced coffee and fresh local pastries in a space that feels like home. We partner with local roasters and bakers to bring you the best in every cup and bite. Whether you're stopping in for your morning pick-me-up or catching up with a friend, we're here to make your day a little better. Come by, say hi, and stay a while.
